NYC Footprints

ABOUT

We’re used to seeing buildings vertically and in relation to everything around them. For this project, I wanted to strip all of that away. To pull each building out of its context and look at it alone. What can the shape of a building tell us when it has nothing around it? This is partly an investigation, partly an excuse to make something that looks cool.

…And here are some of the things I learned about the city I live in from the ground level.
